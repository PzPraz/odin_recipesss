Nasi Goreng

Nasi Goreng is a traditional Indonesian fried rice dish, usually made with pre-cooked rice, garlic, shallots, soy sauce, and other optional ingredients like eggs, shrimp, or chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 shallots, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 egg
  • 100 grams cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sliced cucumber, fried egg, shrimp crackers for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan and saut√© the garlic and shallots until fragrant.
  2. Add the shredded chicken and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  3. Push the ingredients to one side of the pan and scramble the egg on the other side.
  4. Add the cold rice to the pan and mix well with the other ingredients.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and sweet soy sauce, stir until everything is evenly coated.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and fry until the rice is slightly crispy.
  7. Serve with sliced cucumber, fried egg, and shrimp crackers if desired.
