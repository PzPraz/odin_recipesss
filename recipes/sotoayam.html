Soto Ayam

Soto Ayam is a fragrant Indonesian chicken soup made with chicken broth, turmeric, and various spices. It’s served with rice or vermicelli noodles and topped with crispy shallots and lime juice.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams chicken breast
  • 2 liters of water
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 shallots, chopped
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, bruised
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: boiled eggs, fried shallots, lime wedges, vermicelli noodles, sliced cabbage

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken in 2 liters of water until cooked. Remove the chicken and shred it into small pieces, then set aside the broth.
  2. In a pan, heat the vegetable oil and sauté the garlic, shallots, turmeric, and coriander until fragrant.
  3. Add the sautéed spices to the chicken broth along with lemongrass and bay leaves. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Season the broth with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the soup with shredded chicken, boiled eggs, and optional toppings like vermicelli noodles, cabbage, and fried shallots.
  6. Squeeze lime juice over the top before eating for a fresh taste.
